How to play

Press ROLL to pull plastic from the ocean and earn money for the item you find.

Every five minutes, either the robot or the tax payer appears. Click the robot before it leaves to start Frenzy.

During Frenzy, the gold glow means your rolls have much better odds for Epic and Legendary plastic.

If the tax payer appears, click him four times before he reaches your wallet or he steals money.
